# Other female genital (code 36, C51–52, C56–57)

## Quick take
This aggregate includes vulva, vagina, and ovary. Most of the signal is ovarian. The really striking finding is the set of **U-shapes in East Asian women** — Japan Miyagi, Qidong, Hawaii Chinese, Trivandrum, Singapore Indian — all showing a dip at vol II–IX then a pronounced climb. Inland China rising fast (Jiashan, Qidong); Hawaii and LA Chinese women falling from historical highs.

## What catches the eye

- **Japan Miyagi women, U-shape**: 2.46 (vol I) → 2.17 (vol II) → 10.75 (vol XII). Biggest %-change at +337 %. The fall-then-rise is consistent with under-ascertainment + genuine reversal, not coding artefact (other Japanese cancers show the same shape).
- **Hawaii Chinese / LA Chinese women: deep U-shape.** Hawaii Chinese peaked 16.18 (vol II) → 4.04 (vol XI) → 7.27 (vol XII). LA Chinese 13.64 (vol IV) → 6.82 (vol XII). 50 % fall with a weak rebound.
- **India Trivandrum: 19.50 (vol VII) → 5.10 (vol VIII) → 7.95 (vol XII).** As with other sites in this registry, the vol VII → VIII cliff is likely a scope change, not biology.
- **Inland China rising**: Qidong 1.64 → 5.09 (+210 %), Jiashan 2.58 → 6.83 (+165 %). Worth looking separately at ovary vs vulva within this group.
- **Poland Cracow inverted-U peaking vol IX at 16.44** — consistent with other Polish cancer peaks in the 1990s and the subsequent slower decline.
- **Ethnic contrast inside USA and Canada: 7–8×** (vol VII Canadian Asian vs white; vol II–III Israeli Jews Africa/Asia vs non-Jews).
- **Bimodal age curves in Poland Katowice, Kaifeng, South Africa Eastern Cape** — unusual for an aggregate category; likely reflects mixing of ovary (older peak) with vulva (much older) or vagina (bimodal).

## Caveats
- Aggregate site — the sub-components (ovary vs vulva vs vagina) have very different risk profiles. Any finding here must be re-investigated at the sub-site level to be publishable.
- India Trivandrum vol VII–VIII step is almost certainly a registry coding issue.

## Worth a deeper look
- Japan's U-shape for this aggregate mirrors breast, colon, corpus — this "Japanese cancer transition" across five female sites is a coherent story that deserves one chart.
- Decompose the outliers into ovary / vulva / vagina using the sub-site version (C56 / C51-52 / C57) — the Pacific-peoples and inland-China signals likely differ.
