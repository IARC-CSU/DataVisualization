# Oesophagus (code 10, C15)

## Quick take
The world's extreme oesophagus-cancer belt shows up *loud*: three Chinese counties (Cixian, Yangcheng, Yanting) run female ASRs of 60–120 in a world where the median is around 1. At the same time several historical hotspots (Shanghai, Puerto Rico, Singapore Indian) have largely disappeared.

## What catches the eye

- **Cixian County, Hebei is in a class of its own.** Female ASR 123 in vol VIII, 108 in vol X, 102 in vol XI — 97×, 93× and 88× the world female median respectively. No other cancer-registry combination in the dataset gets close to ~100× the median.
- **The Chinese oesophagus belt is visible across registries.** Yangcheng County 85.5 (vol X), Yanting 69.4 (vol XI), Linzhou 61.9 (vol XI), Yanting 58.2 (vol XII). All among the top 10 spatial outliers in CI5.
- **Puerto Rico's collapse is one of the cleanest declines in the dataset.** Female ASR 10.45 (vol I) → 0.50 (vol XII), a 95 % fall; men 23.6 → 3.7, 84 % down. Virtually no inflection — a monotonic historical disappearance.
- **Shanghai men: once 35.5, now 4.9.** A −86 % fall from vol IV to vol XII. Women in Shanghai followed the same path (11.3 → 1.1, −90 %). This is the urbanisation mirror of the inland-China hotspots above.
- **Singapore Indian women collapsed through an inverted-U then down** — 7.55 (vol III) → 8.63 (vol IV) → 0.45 (vol XII). 94 % down. Same Singapore-Indian slide also shows up in oral cavity and in cervix — the subgroup's whole upper-aerodigestive burden is rolling off.
- **Uganda Kyadondo women: ASR 1.16 → 12.98 (vol I → XII).** A 10-fold rise that earns an "erratic" tag because it jumps around. Worth checking against the vol-VII population warning before reading it as a real rise.
- **Ethnic contrasts inside the USA top 38×** — US Black men 22.4 vs Native American 0.6 in vol VI. Black-to-White ratios have compressed somewhat by vol XII but remain the largest in the dataset for this site.

## Caveats
- None of the main findings ride on a coding break.
- Uganda Kyadondo vol VII has a pop_warning; treat the magnitude of the Ugandan female rise with caution.
- The Chinese county registries (Cixian, Yangcheng, Yanting, Linzhou) are special-purpose cancer registries — their denominators are smaller rural populations and ASR is legitimate, but interpret as "pockets of exposure", not national rates.

## Worth a deeper look
- Map Shanghai's 50-year descent alongside Cixian's persistence: a single country showing the full range. Squamous vs adeno split would strengthen the story.
- Puerto Rico's 95 % female fall deserves its own short paper — what changed, and when did the knee occur?
- Is the Ugandan rise real? If yes, it would contradict the general Sub-Saharan pattern and might reflect alcohol and very-hot-beverage exposure.
