# Colon (code 13, C18)

## Quick take
Colon cancer is rising steeply wherever diet is Westernising, and the biggest catch in CI5 is how fast. The "Japan/Korea/Singapore catch-up" is visible as clean inverted-U curves — they overshot Western rates and are now coming back down thanks to screening. Meanwhile many Gulf, African and Indian populations are rising from very low baselines.

## What catches the eye

- **Japan's inverted-U is perfect.** Miyagi Prefecture men 5.45 → 37.90 (vol X) → 30.19; Hiroshima men 11.46 → 59.17 (vol VIII) → 31.44; women in Miyagi 5.95 → 24.02 → 22.0. Both the rise (diet westernisation) and the fall (screening + stabilisation) are in-frame.
- **Singapore Chinese men have overtaken Western rates.** 2.86 (vol I) → 26.49 (vol IX) → 24.28 (vol XII). Women: 2.39 → 20.95 → 19.11. Clean inverted-U peaking around vol IX.
- **Kuwaiti rise from near-zero is extraordinary.** Kuwaiti men 0.19 → 12.66 (+6500 %); women 0.76 → 11.73 (+1443 %). Even allowing for early under-ascertainment the magnitude dwarfs everything else.
- **Algeria / Israel Arabs mirror the Gulf pattern.** Algeria Sétif men 0.73 (vol VI) → 5.48 (vol XI) in a U-shape; Israel Arabs men 5.46 → 19.86 (+264 %).
- **USA Black men are still the highest-rate US group** (41.06 in vol V), but the ethnic spread in the US has narrowed substantially by vol XII (ratio 8× down from 24× in the 1960s).
- **Low-baseline "floor" consists of African, rural-Indian and some Chinese registries.** Uganda Kyadondo men 4.04, Langzhong 3.38, Mumbai 3.1 — around 0.2× the world median.
- **Sex-ratio curiosity in India Chennai (2.5:1 male) and Barshi (3.5:1 male).** Most registries sit near 1.3:1 — the Indian male excess is not universal across India.

## Caveats
- None of the main findings are tied to a coding break for this site.
- Kuwait's earliest CI5 period (vol V–VI) is thought to have had moderate under-ascertainment; the +6500 % magnitude is likely overstated but the direction and final level are robust.

## Worth a deeper look
- Paper idea: "The migrant-catch-up and turn-around of colon cancer in East Asian cities — evidence from CI5 I–XII". Singapore, Hong Kong, Taipei, Osaka, Miyagi fit a single narrative.
- Kuwait's story — from 0.2 to 12 in 40 years — is worth a standalone descriptive piece; the speed is newsworthy.
- Why India Chennai sits at 2.5:1 male while most Indian registries are closer to equality.
