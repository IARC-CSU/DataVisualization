# Non-Hodgkin lymphoma (code 53, C82–85, C96)

## Quick take
NHL is the clearest case in CI5 of an almost-universal post-war rise, driven by a combination of AIDS-related lymphoma (visible as the Zimbabwe Harare African inverted-U), HCV-related lymphomagenesis, pathology-coding changes from ICD-9 to ICD-10, and genuine incidence increase. Israel Jews-born-in-Israel women and India Trivandrum carry historical highs.

## What catches the eye

- **Zimbabwe Harare African men, inverted-U: 4.36 → 16.07 (vol XI) → 13.74.** The rise phase tracks the AIDS epidemic; the partial retreat tracks ART rollout. Visually parallels the Harare eye-cancer curve — same mechanism.
- **Israel Jews-born-in-Israel women vol VII: 21.04, 3.3× median.** One of the highest early NHL rates globally, well before the AIDS era.
- **India Trivandrum women vol VII: 19.14.** Again paired with the vol VII → VIII scope change common to this registry — treat with caution but the vol-VII level is credible.
- **Nordic catch-ups: Finland women +354 %, Iceland women +356 %, Canada Newfoundland men +421 %.** Most Western registries show 3–6× rises across the CI5 window.
- **Bimodal age curves in African registries**: Nigeria Ibadan and Malawi Blantyre show an unusual double peak, with Malawi men having a peak at age band 2 (5–9). This is the Burkitt-lymphoma age distribution — NHL in sub-Saharan Africa includes a paediatric tail that the Western datasets don't have.
- **Malawi Blantyre men vol X, ASR 42.4 at age band 2** — a paediatric Burkitt-lymphoma signal that hit a peak matched almost nowhere else in CI5.
- **Israel Jews-born-in-Africa/Asia men, age-band-1 peak at 40.7** — probably a subset of cases in the paediatric range, consistent with early-onset EBV-related NHL in that cohort.
- **US Black-to-other-ethnic NHL ratios 5–8×** across volumes. Persistent, not narrowing.
- **Malta men +464 %; Singapore Chinese men +626 %; Canadian New Brunswick men +422 %** — Western and Asian catches visible across volumes.

## Caveats
- NHL coding changed substantially between ICD-9 and ICD-10 (C82–85 + C96 captures most but not all lymphoid neoplasms); apparent vol VII–VIII rises may be partly classification.
- Some HIV-related NHL cases may have been captured under other lymphoid sites historically.

## Worth a deeper look
- **The Zimbabwe Harare inverted-U is the clearest HIV/AIDS-cancer-epidemic fingerprint in CI5.** Paired with Kaposi data (not included) it would tell a complete ART-impact story.
- Malawi / Nigeria / Ugandan paediatric Burkitt peaks merit a descriptive update — CI5 XII numbers from Malawi are substantial.
- The Israel-Jews-Israel vol-VII NHL peak is high enough to deserve its own look, likely tied to MALT / gastric lymphoma from H pylori (Israel has high H pylori prevalence historically).
